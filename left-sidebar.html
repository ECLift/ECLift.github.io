Meet the team

We are a diverse and professional team of 24 students, united by our passion for aeronautics. Comprising master’s students in aeronautics and engineering students at Centrale Lyon, we operate like a professional organization, with members divided into specialized departments: Marketing, Propulsion and Avionics, Structure and Manufacturing, and Aerodynamics.

Some of us contributed to the XtraChallenge 2024 competition, where our dedication led us to success. We actively share the knowledge and experience gained from previous years with new members, fostering collaboration and ensuring continuous improvement in our ambitious project.
