ECTanker: Engineered Performance

Versitle, Efficient, Ultralight, Tough

The Mission - XtraChallenge 2025

Short take-off, high rate of climb, efficient glide, fast pylons - this is what the aircraft has been tailored to do. Well, all that plus the ability to carry up to 2L of liquid payload. The ECTanker was made for the Xtra-Challenge 2025, and it demonstrates superior flight performance while adhering to competition rules. You can find the official rules here.

The circuit for the XtraChallege 2025 in Valencia, Spain.

Our Solution

We designed an advanced composite aircraft tailored to the XtraChallenge specifications. Leveraging lightweight materials and efficient aerodynamics, we aim to maximize flight duration and stability. Access our technical report here.

Aerodynamic Design

The aerodynamic design focuses on reducing drag and enhancing lift. The optimized airfoil and high aspect ratio ensure minimal resistance during flight.

Wingspan:2.8 m
Aspect Ratio:16
Root Airfoil:MH-32

Electronic Loadout

The electronics are designed for optimal power management and control. The system includes a primary battery, secondary battery for servos, ESC, UBEC, and multiple servomotors for precise control.
